Places I have worked at

I've worked at the Carrousel and Cue Action in Vlissingen for 2 years. The Carrousel and the Cue Action are owned by the same owners so I worked at both places. Now what are these places I used to work at? Firstly you had the Carrousel which is sort of an indoor fair where you can play games and earn tickets which you can exhange for prizes at the ticket booth. 10 years ago it was the most popular hangout spot in Vlissingen, but that has changed over the course of time.. It's still a great place to have a fun time with your friends and family and if you're ever in Vlissingen I'd recommend you pay a visit to the Carrousel! If you're looking for more information about the Carrousel just click here or on the image below to be transported to the official site.

Now the other branch I worked at is the Cue Action which is a pool and snooker bar. If it isn't obvious enough you can play a game of pool or snooker here, or just have a good time at the bar with a drink or two. There are also two dart boards and you can also order some food here! Working here feels a lot different because it's not aimed at children and teenagers like the Carrousel is. Instead the people that come here are mostly adults but as a young adolescent you can definitely have a good time here too!
